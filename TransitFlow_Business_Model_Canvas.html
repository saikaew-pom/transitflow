Customer Segments
Who We Serve
Type A — In-House Organizer
Marketing Manager, HR Manager, or Executive Assistant at a 200–5,000-person company in SE Asia. Organizes 2–12 corporate events per year (townhalls, offsites, annual parties) with 50–500 attendees requiring transport. Not a transport specialist — coordination is a stressful side task.
Type B — Professional Organizer
Event Director, Coordinator, or Freelance Planner at an event agency or working independently. Manages 10–50+ events per year for corporate clients. Transport is a service they sell and deliver. Reputation depends on flawless execution across multiple complex transport legs per event.
  • Event size: 50–2,000 attendees requiring ground transport
  • Geography: SE Asia primary — Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia
