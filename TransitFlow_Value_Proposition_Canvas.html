Products & Services

TransitFlow Platform

Admin Dashboard Route Builder Driver Portal Passenger Magic Link Live GPS Tracking Email Amendment Flow Digital Boarding Pass Reports & Analytics Daily Automated Reports Project Settings
Pain Relievers

How We Eliminate Frustrations

  • Eliminates WhatsApp chaos — route changes sent via email amendment flow with single-use token confirmation in <60 seconds
  • No more manual headcounts — QR check-in and digital boarding pass automate attendance tracking
  • Ends blind spots — passengers and managers see live GPS on any device, no app download required
  • Kills coordination overload — one dashboard replaces spreadsheets, calls, and 5 different messaging threads
  • Removes audit risk — timestamped event log for every trip, driver, and passenger — export-ready for compliance
Gain Creators

How We Create Desired Outcomes

  • Saves ~8–10 hrs/week per operations coordinator — from scheduling, communication, and tracking tasks
  • Delivers cost visibility — utilisation rates and cost-per-seat data to justify the programme to Finance
  • Boosts employee satisfaction — reliable, on-time shuttle with real-time ETA improves commute NPS
  • ESG-ready reporting — CO₂ per trip, fleet utilisation, and headcount data for sustainability submissions
  • Scales without extra headcount — manage 5 or 50 vehicles from the same platform, same team
Customer Jobs

What They Need to Get Done

  • Functional: Plan and publish daily/weekly shuttle routes and schedules
  • Functional: Communicate schedule changes to drivers and passengers reliably
  • Functional: Track attendance and ensure no passenger is left behind
  • Functional: Report programme cost and utilisation to HR and Finance leadership
  • Social: Be seen as a competent, proactive operations leader
  • Emotional: Feel in control — not reactive — when things change last minute
Pains

Frustrations & Risks They Face

  • Spending hours every week coordinating over WhatsApp and fixing missed messages
  • No real-time visibility — passengers have no idea where their shuttle is or if it's delayed
  • Manual attendance tracking on spreadsheets — updated after the fact, often inaccurate
  • Cannot justify programme cost to Finance — no data on utilisation or cost per seat
  • Driver no-shows and last-minute substitutions cause chaos with no fallback system
  • Fear of a passenger incident or complaint escalating with no audit trail to defend decisions
Gains

Outcomes & Desires They Want

  • Peace of mind — knowing every route is running smoothly without constant check-ins
  • Management confidence — able to show Finance the numbers that justify the shuttle programme
  • Happy employees — reliable, on-time shuttles with real-time ETA reduce commute stress
  • Time back — reclaim hours spent on manual coordination for more strategic work
  • ESG credibility — data to demonstrate the company's commitment to sustainable commuting
