Pain Relievers

How We Eliminate Frustrations

  • AB Kills event-day WhatsApp floods — every attendee gets a magic link with live GPS; no calls or messages to the organizer
  • AB Route changes in 60 seconds — email amendment flow with single-use token; all passengers and drivers notified instantly
  • AB Real-time boarding visibility — QR check-in and digital boarding pass; live headcount on the admin dashboard
  • B Tames multi-leg complexity — manage 10 transport legs, multiple vehicles and drivers per event from one screen
  • A Justifies transport spend — per-event cost, utilisation, and headcount report ready for Finance in 2 clicks
  • AB Full audit trail — timestamped log of every boarding, route change, and driver action; exportable for incident review
Gain Creators

How We Create Desired Outcomes

  • AB Stress-free event day — organizer focuses on the event, not transport. Zero firefighting because passengers are already informed
  • B Premium client experience — branded, seamless transport communication reflects the quality of your event delivery and wins repeat business
  • A Looks great to leadership — no transport complaints escalating after the event; professional execution visible to senior management
  • AB Time back — reclaim 2–4 hours per event previously spent on manual coordination and last-minute communication
  • B Scalable without extra headcount — handle 3× the event volume with the same team; no extra coordinator needed
  • A ESG & duty-of-care data — passenger logs and CO₂ estimates per event for sustainability and HR compliance reporting
Customer Jobs

What They Need to Get Done

  • AB Functional: Plan routes, assign drivers and vehicles for every transport leg of an event
  • AB Functional: Communicate pickup details, ETAs, and schedule changes to all attendees reliably
  • AB Functional: Track real-time headcount and boarding status during the event
  • A Functional: Report event transport costs and utilisation to HR/Finance after the event
  • B Functional: Deliver a polished, on-brand transport experience that reflects well on the agency
  • A Social: Be seen as a competent, well-organised coordinator by peers and leadership
  • B Social: Build a reputation as a reliable, premium event partner who handles every detail
  • AB Emotional: Feel calm and in control on event day — not reactive and overwhelmed
Pains

Frustrations & Risks They Face

  • AB Event day is consumed by "Where is my shuttle?" messages and calls from attendees
  • AB Last-minute changes (venue swap, driver no-show, route update) require calling or messaging every affected attendee individually
  • AB No live boarding visibility — organizer has no idea who has boarded or which vehicle they are on during the event
  • B Managing 5–10 transport legs per event across multiple vehicles, drivers, and pickup points is extremely complex and error-prone
  • A Cannot justify transport costs to management or Finance — no data on utilisation or cost per head after the event
  • B A transport failure damages client relationship and risks losing repeat business — reputation is always on the line
  • AB No audit trail if a passenger incident, complaint, or no-show dispute arises after the event
Gains

Outcomes & Desires They Want

  • AB Calm event day — transport runs in the background; organizer stays focused on the event itself
  • A Recognition from leadership — flawless transport execution is noticed and appreciated by senior management
  • B Client delight & repeat business — guests experience seamless, professional transport that reflects the agency's quality
  • AB Proof of what happened — a post-event report showing boarding counts, on-time performance, and cost data
  • AB Time back — reclaim hours previously spent on manual coordination and last-minute communication
  • B Competitive edge — able to offer "digital transport management" as a differentiating service to prospective clients
